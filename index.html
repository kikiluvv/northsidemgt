About

Land Use Planning & Development Management is the cornerstone of every successful project regardless of use, size or complexity. NorthSide Management specializes in bridging the gap between owners, designers, jurisdictions and contractors ensuring project needs are met while retaining budgets & timelines. With over 20 years of experience in Residential, Commercial, Retail, & Industrial Land Development we can tailor to your project's specific needs by offering the following services throughout each stage in your project:

· Feasibility /Pre-Development
· Entitlement / Design Management
· Procurement / Cost Accounting
· Construction Management
· Post Construction & Turnover

Projects

"Will your project stand financially?"

The Pre-Development and Feasibility stages of your project are vital in establishing information that can weigh this question. NorthSide Management is experienced with establishing concept designs, time/cost forecasting, & providing accurate feasibility studies.

During your projects entitlement & design, NorthSide Management brings construction knowledge & cost association that guides the project vision around real world constructability. This philosophy produces upfront knowledge of a project's budget, approvability, schedule, & value engineering possibilities.
